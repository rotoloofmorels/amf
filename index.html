About

India's premier Formula Student Team, 15 years strong.

Ashwa Mobility Foundation is India’s premier Formula SAE team, participating out of R.V College of Engineering, Bangalore. Formula SAE event is one where SAE student members conceive, design, fabricate and compete with other similar formula-style cars.

  • 123 Members
  • 56 Prototypes
  • 23 Projects
